# ☕ Support the Repository

Thank you for visiting the Johnson City Civic Repository. This platform was built to provide independent, transparent, and easy-to-navigate information regarding local zoning, infrastructure, and community development. 

### Why Support This Project?
Running a public data repository requires ongoing maintenance, hosting resources, and data gathering. If you have found these timelines, data center studies, or transit toolkits helpful for navigating our city's growth, consider supporting the effort. 

Your support directly covers:
*   Digital domain and web hosting maintenance fees.
*   The time spent compiling complex municipal budgets, public hearing notes, and regional planning studies.
*   Keeping this entire platform completely ad-free and open to every resident.
