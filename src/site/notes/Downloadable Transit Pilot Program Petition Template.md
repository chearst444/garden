Petition to the City Commission of Johnson City, Tennessee

Regarding: Funding a Pilot Program for Extended Fixed-Route Transit Hours

Statement of Purpose:

We, the undersigned residents and stakeholders of Johnson City, petition the City Commission to allocate operational funding for a pilot program extending Johnson City Transit (JCT) fixed-route operations from 6:15 PM to 10:00 PM on weekdays.

Reliable evening transportation is a critical pillar of community health, public safety, and economic equity. Expanding these hours ensures that shift workers in our local retail, hospitality, grocery, and healthcare sectors have safe, dependable, and affordable transit home. Furthermore, it bridges the gap between our higher education student bodies and the evening economy, reduces impaired driving, and maximizes the utility of daytime transit investments by eliminating the psychological barrier of the "stranded rider."

By signing below, we urge city leadership to prioritize basic operational mobility alongside our community's recreational and infrastructure investments.
